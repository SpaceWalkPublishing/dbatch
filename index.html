Let's Work Together

It is easy to moan about the state of the world, and hard to find ways to improve it.
Empathy, education, and engagement are all essential to finding our way forward.

We must commit ourselves, one and all, to making a difference. We must be courageous.
We must vote. We must speak. We must volunteer our time. We must guide those struggling to
understand the perils of a scientifically illiterate society. And, most importantly, we must
strongly support anyone who can make a difference.

Whatever your background or personal opinions, I hope we can find some
common ground and work together to help improve everyone's quality of life.

That's me, on a mountain, about to do one of my jobs.
